# Figure and Table Captions

Draft captions for the figures and tables generated by the analysis pipeline (`analysis/build_report.py`). These captions are written in a neutral academic style and can be adapted directly into the dissertation. Replace `[DATE_RANGE]` with the actual evaluation period.

---

## Figure captions

### sessions_started_vs_completed.png

**Figure [N].** Daily sessions started and completed during the evaluation period ([DATE_RANGE]). A session is counted as "started" when the user opened and interacted with it (i.e., `started_at` was recorded). A session is counted as "completed" when all assigned stages were finished. Each day contributes at most one session.

---

### session_completion_rate.png

**Figure [N].** Cumulative session completion rate over the evaluation period. The rate is calculated as the running proportion of started sessions that reached the completed state. Only sessions with a recorded `started_at` timestamp are included in the denominator; auto-created sessions that were never opened are excluded.

---

### flashcard_accuracy.png

**Figure [N].** Daily flashcard attempt accuracy over the evaluation period. The solid line shows the proportion of correct attempts across all submitted flashcard attempts (including both main-queue and retry-queue attempts). The dashed line shows the review-card correctness rate (correctness on review-card attempts only), used as a behavioural proxy for retention. Days with no flashcard attempts are omitted.

---

### new_card_attempts.png

**Figure [N].** New-card main-queue attempts per day during the evaluation period. Each bar represents the count of submitted flashcard attempts from the main queue where the queue kind was "new" on that session date. This counts submitted attempts, not unique words encountered.

---

### review_card_attempts.png

**Figure [N].** Review-card main-queue attempts per day during the evaluation period. Each bar represents the count of submitted flashcard attempts from the main queue where the queue kind was "review" on that session date. This counts submitted attempts, not unique words reviewed. Retry-queue attempts are excluded.

---

### total_attempts.png

**Figure [N].** Total flashcard attempts per day during the evaluation period. The lighter bars show the total count of all submitted attempts (both main-queue and retry-queue). The darker overlaid bars show the subset of retry-queue attempts, which are re-presentations of incorrectly answered cards within the same session.

---

### retry_attempts.png

**Figure [N].** Retry-queue flashcard attempts per day during the evaluation period. Each bar represents the count of submitted retry attempts (cards re-presented within the same session after an incorrect response). Retries that were scheduled but not answered are not included.

---

### logged_active_time.png

**Figure [N].** Logged active time per day during the evaluation period, stacked by modality. Flashcard time is the sum of per-attempt response times. Reading and listening time are client-recorded active seconds. This measure represents a lower bound on engagement time and excludes idle time, navigation between sections, and abandoned views.

---

### reading_completions.png

**Figure [N].** Reading stage completions per day during the evaluation period. Each bar indicates whether the reading stage was marked complete on that session date (0 or 1). Reading stage completion is a binary flag indicating that the user progressed through the reading step of the daily loop; it does not measure reading comprehension or the amount of text read.

---

### listening_completions.png

**Figure [N].** Listening stage completions per day during the evaluation period. Each bar indicates whether the listening stage was marked complete on that session date (0 or 1). Listening stage completion is a binary flag indicating that the user progressed through the listening step; it does not measure listening comprehension or the proportion of audio content consumed.

---

### saved_words.png

**Figure [N].** Reader-saved words per day during the evaluation period. Each bar represents the count of words saved from the interactive reader into the user's review deck on that session date. Duplicate saves of the same word are ignored (append-only). Saving a word indicates the user chose to add it for future review, not that the word was learned or retained.

---

### days_active_rolling.png

**Figure [N].** Days with recorded activity within a 7-day rolling window during the evaluation period. A day is counted as active if at least one flashcard attempt was submitted, a word was saved from the reader, or a reading or listening stage was completed. The y-axis shows the count of active days in the preceding 7-day window for each date.

---

### stage_completion.png

**Figure [N].** Number of sessions reaching each stage milestone during the evaluation period. From left to right: sessions with a recorded start, sessions where the flashcard stage was completed, sessions where the reading stage was completed, sessions where the listening stage was completed (only counted when a listening asset was assigned), and sessions that were fully completed. The decrease across stages represents the cumulative drop-off at each milestone.

---

### review_correctness_proxy.png

**Figure [N].** Review-card correctness over time during the evaluation period. Each point shows the proportion of correct attempts among review-card attempts (queue kind = "review") on that session date. This metric is used as a behavioural proxy for retention. It measures in-app response correctness and does not directly assess recall outside the application, transfer to naturalistic contexts, or long-term retention. Days with no review-card attempts are omitted.

---

## Table captions

### summary_metrics.csv / summary_metrics.md

**Table [N].** Summary metrics for the evaluation period ([DATE_RANGE]). Each row presents a single metric with its computed value. Definitions and limitations for each metric are provided in the Measures section. The review-card correctness metric is a behavioural proxy for retention and is labelled accordingly.

---

### daily_aggregates.csv

**Table [N].** Daily aggregates for the evaluation period. Each row represents one session date and includes session completion status, flashcard attempt counts (total, new-card, review-card, and retry), flashcard accuracy, review-card correctness (retention proxy), reader-saved word counts, reading and listening stage completion flags, and logged active time broken down by modality. This table can be used for further analysis or included as an appendix.

---

### metric_definitions.md

**Table [N].** Data dictionary of metric definitions as exported from the application. Each row specifies a metric identifier, label, description, and known limitations. This table is generated from the machine-readable definitions embedded in the export bundle and reflects the exact metric semantics used to produce all figures and summary values.
